6 Tips for Emails That Are Guaranteed To Get Responses keywords: warm up your cold email list, best practices for sending emails

With the rise of social media, cold emails have become increasingly less common in business communication. But when used correctly, they can be an effective way to introduce yourself and your company to potential customers. Here are some tips for optimizing your email outreach:

  1. Pick the right time of day: Emails seem more urgent in the morning and evening, so you’ll get a better response rate if you email people around those times.

  2. Add a personal touch: Make it clear you’ve read their profile by mentioning something from it in your first sentence or two.

  3. Keep your subject line concise: People will be more likely to open your email if they know what it is about from just reading the title of it, so don’t make them guess!
